MINI x The Devil Wears Prada Musical

Client: MINI

For the West End premiere of The Devil Wears Prada: A New Musical, MINI's brand activation took centre stage on the red carpet and at the gala afterparty at the British Museum. The installation featured the iconic Giant Stiletto with a devil's tail heel, alongside large 3D lettering that created a striking backdrop for the event.


I was responsible for both the 3D modelling and the technical design, ensuring the bold concept could be fabricated and installed seamlessly for this high-profile celebration.
