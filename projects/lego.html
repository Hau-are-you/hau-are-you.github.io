LEGO F1 Minifigure Helmet

Client: LEGO

For this project I was responsible for the 3D modelling and technical design of a series of large-scale LEGO F1 minifigure helmets created for events around the world. Each helmet was developed from 3D-printed master patterns that were used to produce GRP moulds for the head and cap, with the design allowing the helmet to be comfortably worn.


Additional components such as the perforated visor and detachable neck collar were SLA printed and attached using internal magnets for easy removal. The models were finished in custom paint colours with hand-detailed faces to replicate LEGO's distinctive look.
