Giant Xbox

Client: Square Enix

For the launch of Final Fantasy XVI, I was responsible for the 3D modelling and technical design of a giant Xbox installation created in collaboration with Square Enix. Standing 3 metres tall, the oversized console was built as a highly accurate replica, complete with a fully functioning power button featuring LED push lighting and custom vinyl overlays.


The structure was developed around a timber frame with CNC-cut MDF panels, the perforated detailing is a foam panel top coated with polyurethane for durability. Ports and other features were recreated with 3D-printed components.


The final installation featured in a promotional video campaign for the title.
