EA FC Pro Finals Trophy

Client: Electronic Arts

In 2023, I was commissioned to design a pair of trophies for the EA FC Pro Open. This project marked my first collaboration with EA, focusing solely on product design.


As the designer of the trophies, I was responsible for the entire process from concept through to technical detailing. The design was inspired by the game's visual identity, which features strong triangular elements, as well as its emphasis on fluid, dynamic gameplay — both of which shaped the form of the trophies.


I developed several proposals based on EA FC's brand guidelines before refining the chosen concept into detailed 3D CAD models. These models served as the blueprint for fabrication, ensuring all components could be manufactured and assembled with precision.


The final trophies were produced using SLS 3D printing technology, divided into separate components and finished with individual paintwork. Two versions were realised from my designs: a 500mm trophy for the winner, and a larger 600mm version to be presented annually at the finals. While the fabrication and finishing were handled by the workshop team, my role centred on transforming EA's vision into a production-ready design.
