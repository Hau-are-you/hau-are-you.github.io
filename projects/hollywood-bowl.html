Hollywood Bowl

Client: Stripe Communications

For the launch of Hollywood Bowl's new Uxbridge venue, I was responsible for the 3D modelling and technical design of a larger-than-life installation created as a bold PR stunt. The concept featured a giant pink bowling ball dropped onto the bonnet of a real car, designed to be immediate, oversized, and impossible to ignore.


The final piece was built and installed on site as a striking photo opportunity and brand activation, successfully drawing attention and engagement at street level.
