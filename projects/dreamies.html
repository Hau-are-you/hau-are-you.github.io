Dreamies “Ad Attack” Billboard

Client: Dreamies

For the Dreamies “Ad Attack” campaign, oversized 3D cats were created to dramatically claw across billboards, scale rooftops, and cling to drainpipes in their playful hunt for treats. These striking fibreglass sculptures, hand-painted and based on real cat poses, became a bold presence in both out-of-home and social media activations.


I was responsible for the digital sculpting of the cats and the technical design, ensuring the poses were both visually dynamic and structurally feasible for large-scale fabrication.
