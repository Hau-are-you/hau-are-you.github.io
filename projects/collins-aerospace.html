Collins Aerospace: Actuation Systems

For this project I was responsible for both the technical design and the 3D modelling.


Working closely with Collins Aerospace Actuation Systems, I created a model that effectively demonstrated the capabilities of their actuation systems.


The final piece was well received by the client and is now being used as a tool to support their presentations and communications.
