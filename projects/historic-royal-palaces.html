Halloween Experience

Client: Hampton Court Palace

For the Halloween season at Hampton Court Palace, our team at Scruffy Dog Creative Group created an immersive haunted experience filled with atmospheric sets, animatronics, lighting, and sound effects. The project combined creativity with historical sensitivity, ensuring the palace's heritage was preserved while bringing its ghostly stories to life.

I contributed to the project as part of the technical design team, developing practical solutions that transformed creative concepts into buildable designs, helping deliver an engaging and seamless visitor experience.
