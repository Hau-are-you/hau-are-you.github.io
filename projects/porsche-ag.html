Wind Turbine Model

Client: Porsche AG

For this project I was responsible for both the technical design and the 3D modelling of a 1.7m kinetic wind turbine created for Porsche AG's sustainability exhibit in their Piccadilly showroom in London. Starting from the STL file provided by the client, I redesigned the model in Rhino to make it fabrication-ready.

The turbine was constructed with fibreglass, a welded steel frame for structural support, and 3D-printed blades mounted on an MDF base. The final model featured motorised blades with adjustable speed and an accessible control panel, finished in Porsche's specified RAL6025 matt colour. This installation is currently on display as part of Porsche's commitment to sustainability and green energy.
