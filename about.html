Product Designer

Technical Designer | 3D Modeller

I am a Technical Designer and 3D Modeller, specialising in transforming creative ideas into tangible, buildable outcomes. My work focuses on bridging the gap between concept and fabrication, ensuring every design is both visually compelling and technically feasible.

I graduated in 2020 with a degree in Product Design from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, and since then I have worked on a wide range of projects, from large-scale installations and brand activations to detailed scale models. Collaborating with global brands across entertainment, automotive, retail, and cultural sectors, I bring precision, problem-solving, and craft to every project.

My approach is grounded in clarity and technical detail, while always respecting the original creative vision.
